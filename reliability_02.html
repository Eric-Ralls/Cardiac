Autoantibodies are more prevalent and clear during an event

Autoantibodies are triggered or boosted in acute cardiac events and often target the midfragment of the Troponin I which is usually the sole detection site of Troponin I assays.30

Results of Troponin I tests are not always interchangeable or comparable because of the differences in antibody reactivity.31
