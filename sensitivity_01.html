Criteria for Acute MI

Detection of a rise and/or fall of cardiac biomarkers values with at least one value above the 99th percentile upper reference limit (URL). 6

Troponins are essential in the diagnosis of AMI, according to the Third Definition released in August 2012, as clear evidence of necrosis. A rise or fall pattern and at least one value above the 99th percentile are essential for Troponins.
