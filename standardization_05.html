Correlation of TnI results

TnI POC - 0.03 ng/mL
(Negative - 99th percentile of 0.08 ng/mL)

TnI Core Lab - 0.05 ng/mL
(Positive - 99th percentile of 0.04 ng/mL)

Let's say a patient comes in to the ED having an event. A POC Troponin I test is run and the patient is negative at 0.03 ng/mL. A second test is run using a different Troponin I test at the core lab (using a 99th percentile of 0.04 ng/mL) and the result comes back positive at 0.05 ng/mL.

Even though each of these tests were run on the same patient specimen, the results do not correlate.

As you can see, no Troponin I assays correlate, and therefore there is no standardization.
