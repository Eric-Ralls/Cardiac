Immunoassay Sandwich Technique 2,5

All cardiac troponin assays require the use of the immunoassay technique.

These assays have 3 components.

  • They have a capture antibody which sits down first and begins looking for Troponin.
  • The Troponin then adheres to the capture antibody.
  • Finally, the detection antibody is looking for the Troponin.

Once the detection antibody adheres to the Troponin, making the complete sandwich, a signal is given off.

This signal is then measured to determine the amount of Troponin in the blood.
