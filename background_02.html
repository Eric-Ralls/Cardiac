What is Troponin? 2,3

Troponin is a globular protein complex attached to actin filament of striated muscle, distributed at regular intervals along the tropomyosin filament. The complex has three subunits.4

As a result of its high tissue-specificity, cardiac troponin (cTn) is a cardio-specific, highly sensitive marker for myocardial damage.

There are two types of cTn:

  • cTnI
  • cTnT
